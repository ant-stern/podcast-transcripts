About Podcast Transcripts:

This site provides searchable databases of AI-generated transcripts for various podcasts and radio shows.

I aim to make the archives as complete as I can. I use the newest, most powerful faster-whisper model for transcription. It's quite accurate but may struggle with proper nouns or made-up words. Get creative with your spelling if you feel like you're missing results.

I am a one man operation, so please be patient as I continue to improve and expand the site. If you have any questions, concerns, or want to request a show: I'm on social media @ant_stern and you can e-mail me at fleaowns@gmail.com.
